Background

I spent a decade in enterprise SaaS before founding SharpPages. I sold software at Salesforce, ran customer success at Microsoft, and helped scale Datajoy, a data startup that was acquired by Databricks. Along the way, I earned my MBA at UC Berkeley Haas.

SharpPages started after I kept seeing the same pattern. Businesses paying agencies $150 to $300 per hour for sites that score 70 on PageSpeed, take 8 weeks to deliver, and come with change orders for every revision. The sites were slow. The SEO was an afterthought. The bills kept growing.

I decided to build sites the way they should be built. Static HTML that loads in under a second. Programmatic SEO that generates hundreds of targeted pages. Ad campaigns with full attribution from impression to conversion. The results spoke for themselves.

What I Build

Fast Static Sites

Static HTML that loads in under a second. 98 PageSpeed score across 700+ pages. No WordPress, no Webflow, no recurring platform costs.

Programmatic SEO

Build scripts that generate hundreds of keyword-targeted pages from structured data. 363K impressions in 30 days for one client, from near-zero.

Event + Ad Campaigns

Multi-city event registration sites with full attribution. Custom audience targeting that delivers 62% lower ad costs than industry average.
