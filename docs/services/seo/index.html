Frequently Asked Questions

What is programmatic SEO?

Building hundreds or thousands of pages from structured data. Each page targets a specific keyword with unique content. We built 398 pages for one client and 322 for another. Both rank on page one for their target terms.

How long until I see results?

PE Collective hit 363K impressions in 30 days. Most projects see meaningful traffic within 2 to 6 weeks of launch, depending on domain authority and keyword difficulty.

Do you write the content?

We generate structured content from data. Each page has unique titles, descriptions, and body content targeting specific search intents. This is not AI-generated blog spam. It is structured, keyword-targeted content at scale.

Can I manage SEO myself after launch?

Yes. We hand you the build system, content architecture, and all files. You can expand it yourself or hire us for monthly management.
