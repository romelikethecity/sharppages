Frequently Asked Questions

Will my design change?

Only if you want it to. We can replicate your current design pixel-for-pixel on a static stack, or improve it during migration. Your call.

What about my existing SEO rankings?

We set up 301 redirects for every URL, preserve canonical tags, and carry over all meta data. Your rankings are protected.

How long does a migration take?

Most migrations take 2 to 4 weeks depending on page count and content complexity. We give you a fixed timeline upfront.

What if I have a custom CMS?

We can migrate from any platform. Custom CMS migrations start at $5,000 and depend on the complexity of the content structure.
