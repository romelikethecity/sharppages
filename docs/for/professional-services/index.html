Web Design & SEO for Professional Services

Accounting firms, consultancies, financial advisors, insurance brokers. Your website is a credibility check. Every prospect visits your site before taking a meeting. If it looks like a 2018 Squarespace template with 5 pages, they are forming impressions before you speak.

The Problem

Your Squarespace site has 5 pages, scores 55 on mobile, and the blog has not been updated in years.

You rank nowhere for '[service] + [city].' Competitors with 30+ content pages are outranking you.

Your competitors hired an agency at $150/hr. You need the same credibility at a fraction of the cost.

Why Professional Services Need a Different Approach

Professional services firms win on credibility. Your website is the first thing a prospect checks after a referral. A fast, well-designed site with content depth signals competence and investment in your practice.

Service and industry vertical pages are the SEO foundation. A CPA firm serving restaurants, real estate investors, and startups should have separate pages for each. Each targets specific search queries and demonstrates specialized expertise.

Most professional services firms underinvest in web presence. Meanwhile, competitors with better sites capture prospects who search before asking for referrals. The firm that ranks first for 'business attorney Denver' gets the call.

Page speed signals professionalism. A consulting firm advising on operational efficiency should not have a 4-second load time. A sub-1-second site demonstrates the attention to detail clients expect.
