Web Design & SEO for Medical Device Companies

Medical device companies run field marketing events, maintain product websites, and need to rank for clinical search terms. Most are stuck with agency-built WordPress sites that score 60 on mobile PageSpeed and cost $5,000 per year in hosting and maintenance.

The Problem

Your corporate site loads in 4 seconds on mobile. Physicians evaluating your products on their phones between patients close the tab before the page renders.

Compliance review cycles add weeks to every agency deliverable. By the time the landing page is approved, you have lost half the promotion window.

You run 10 to 20 field events per year across multiple cities. Each one needs a registration page, tracking, and an ad campaign. Starting from scratch every time compounds the overhead.

Why Medical Device Companies Need a Different Approach

Medical device marketing has a credibility problem online. Your products go through years of clinical trials and FDA clearance, but your website looks like it was built on a Squarespace template in 2019. Physicians evaluating your products form an impression of your company before they ever speak to a rep. A slow, dated site undermines the clinical credibility your products earned.

Field marketing events are the backbone of device sales. Physician dinners, KOL presentations, product demos, city-by-city lunch-and-learns. Each event needs a registration page that communicates the same quality as the event itself. Generic Eventbrite pages with default styling do not meet that standard for a premium physician dinner at a five-star hotel.

The template cloning model is built for how device companies operate. You run the same event format in Minneapolis, then Detroit, then Dallas. Each city gets a fresh site with local details, but the design, tracking architecture, and proven copy structure carry over. By the fourth city, your cost per event site drops to a fraction of the first.

SEO for medical devices targets specific clinical terms, product comparisons, and specialty-focused content. Programmatic SEO generates hundreds of pages from structured data, each targeting a specific search intent. The same approach that drove 363K impressions in 30 days for PE Collective works for device companies targeting surgical specialties, orthopedics, or dermatology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you work within pharma/device compliance requirements?

Yes. You send approved copy, or we draft copy for your compliance team to review. The build does not go live until your team signs off. We are used to revision cycles.

Do you build on WordPress?

No. Static HTML sites load faster, score higher on PageSpeed, and have zero plugin vulnerabilities. No CMS maintenance. Your site files live in a repository you control.

Can you handle multi-city event rollouts?

Yes. We build the first event site as a template. Each additional city clones in 48 hours with local venue, date, and contact list details swapped in. Same design, separate tracking per city.

What PageSpeed scores do your sites achieve?

90+ on mobile, consistently. Our benchmark site (getprovyx.com) scores 98 with a 0.9s Speed Index across 398 pages. Run it through PageSpeed Insights yourself.
